About Me

Hi, I am Vimal Raj

  • Productivity Addict
  • Highly Geeky
  • Self Taught Programmer

Self-improvement has been an obsession of mine for as long as I can remember. Everything that I do, be it leveling up in a video-game or writing an essay, I have an irresistible urge to find the perfect way to do it. Sometimes this can be overwhelming but for a teenager in his finals years of school, it is an extremely valuable skill. That is exactly why I want to share it with the world.

Along with geeking out about productivity systems, I code in my free time. This website was built entirely out of Django (a python based framework). I also love to talk and share my views with people - hence the blog. Oh, and literature. I read a lot. Most of my ideas form out of books I've read.

School and college overwhelms a lot of teenagers. Time starts vanishing into thin air and blood slowly turns into caffeine. Balancing academics, work and extra-cirrculars is not an easy task. Here, productivity comes in as the saviour. Learning how to learn is something a lot of students look past and in this era of distractions, it is essential. The Productive Geek (ahem, that's me) will help fill this knowledge gap.
